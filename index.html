Powerful Search

Search Every Dimension
of a Criminal Case

Cross-reference across courts, jurisdictions, offence types, and time periods. One interface to Australia’s entire criminal law landscape.

Search by Name

Find any defendant, appellant, or party across all Australian courts. Search full names, partial matches, or phonetic variants using our advanced text engine. Results include linked case histories and related proceedings.

By Year & Era

Filter by specific year or date range. From colonial-era proceedings to today’s High Court decisions.

By Court Level

Supreme Court, District Court, Magistrates Court, High Court — browse by judicial level or drill into specific court registries.

By Offence Type

Filter by specific charges — from minor offences to serious indictable crimes. Includes offence classifications across all states.

By Jurisdiction

State-by-state or territory-wide. Compare how different jurisdictions have handled similar offences across time.

Full Judgment Text & Citations

Access complete judgment documents with official citations, catchwords, and linked references. Every case cross-referenced with related proceedings, appeal history, and subsequent citations. Export-ready for legal research.
