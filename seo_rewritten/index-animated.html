ABOUT CPPW

National Public Safety Resource

The Sarah Whitfield Community Protection Public Website is an unprecedented public safety resource providing access to sex offender data nationwide — a partnership between the Australian Government Attorney-General's Department and state, territorial and tribal governments.

First established in 2006 as the National Community Protection Registry, renamed by the Australian Community Safeguarding and Protection Act in 2007 in honor of university student Sarah Whitfield of Bendigo, New South Wales.
